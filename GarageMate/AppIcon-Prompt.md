# App Icon Prompt

## Final prompt
“Create a premium iOS app icon for an app called GarageMate, a car maintenance companion. The icon should be a 1024×1024 rounded-square composition with an iOS 26 Liquid Glass-inspired style. Use a deep glossy translucent background with subtle depth, soft reflections, and glass-like highlights. In the center, show a minimal modern car silhouette inside a subtle garage outline. Add a small maintenance badge in the lower-right corner, such as a simple wrench or checkmark, but keep it minimal and recognizable at small sizes. The icon should feel Apple-native, premium, clean, and friendly. No text, no real car brand logos, no tiny details, no photorealistic car, no clutter. It should look good in both light and dark mode and feel like it belongs next to Apple Wallet, Reminders, and Fitness.”

## Alternative 1
“Create a premium 1024×1024 iOS app icon for GarageMate. Design a Liquid Glass-style service gauge with a minimal car silhouette in the center and a tiny wrench indicator. Use translucent layers, soft highlights, depth, and a polished Apple-native look. No text, no brand logos, no clutter.”

## Alternative 2
“Create a premium 1024×1024 iOS app icon for GarageMate. Design a minimal car silhouette inside a glass shield or vault shape to represent protected car maintenance records. Use an iOS 26 Liquid Glass-inspired style with soft depth, clean gradients, and high recognizability at small sizes. No text, no brand logos, no clutter.”

## Concept decision
Final choice: Concept A — Glass Garage.

Rationale: Concept A has the clearest small-size read because the garage outline, car silhouette, and small maintenance badge communicate the app’s purpose immediately without brand marks or tiny detail.
