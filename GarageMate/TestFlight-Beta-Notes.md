# TestFlight Beta Notes

## App name
GarageMate

## Version
1.0 beta

## Short beta description
GarageMate is a simple and beautiful car maintenance companion for tracking service history, oil changes, insurance, inspections, reminders, mechanic notes, and repair costs.

## What to test
- Sign in with Apple
- Creating a user profile
- Adding a car
- Selecting make, model, and year
- Uploading a car photo
- Adding service records
- Adding oil change records
- Adding insurance records
- Adding inspection records
- Adding mechanic notes
- Creating reminders
- Receiving local notifications
- Marking reminders as complete
- Viewing total repair costs
- Switching preferred currency between EUR and USD
- Switching mileage unit between km and mi
- Deleting cars, records, notes, and reminders
- Dark mode
- Large text / Dynamic Type

## Beta feedback questions
- Was adding your first car easy?
- Did the app feel simple or too detailed?
- Was adding a service record fast enough?
- Were reminders clear?
- Was anything confusing?
- What feature would you want next?

## Known limitations
- Data is stored locally using SwiftData.
- iCloud sync is not included in version 1.
- Live currency conversion is not included in version 1.
- Receipt scanning/OCR is not included in version 1.

## Privacy summary
GarageMate stores car maintenance data locally on the device using SwiftData. Version 1 does not use a backend server. Sign in with Apple is used for account identity. Car photos and receipt images are stored locally.
